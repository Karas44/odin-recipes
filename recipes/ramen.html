Its Ramen

Description

Who doesn't eat instant ramen? Just in case you are in such a state as to forget how to make it, I got you covered. Let's begin.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet your choice flavor of ramen
  • Water

Steps

  1. Open ramen packet and pour ramen noodles into bowl. Boil water.
  2. Once water is boiling, pour over noodles. Cover and wait 3 minutes.
  3. Uncover, then stir in flavor packet.
  4. Enjoy!
